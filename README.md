## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I use the app?

1. Open the app and allow camera access
2. Point your camera at the solar panel
3. The app will measure light intensity and calculate power output

### Why does the app need camera access?

The app uses the camera to measure ambient light intensity, which is essential for calculating solar panel power output.

### What solar panels are supported?

The app supports various types of solar panels. You can customize panel parameters in the settings.

## Troubleshooting

### App crashes when opening camera

- Make sure you have granted camera permissions in Settings
- Restart the app and try again

### Measurement seems inaccurate

- Ensure you are measuring under direct sunlight
- Check that your solar panel parameters are correctly set

## Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. The camera is only used for light measurement and no images are stored or transmitted.
