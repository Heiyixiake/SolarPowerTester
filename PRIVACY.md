# Privacy Policy for SolarPowerTester

Last updated: March 10, 2026

## Information We Collect

SolarPowerTester does not collect any personal information. The app only uses your device's camera to measure light intensity for calculating solar panel power output.

## Camera Usage

- The camera is used solely for measuring ambient light intensity
- No images or video are stored, transmitted, or shared
- All processing is done locally on your device

## Data Storage

- No user data is stored on external servers
- All calculations are performed locally on your device
